Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

"No, modern root canal treatments are performed under anesthesia, making the procedure virtually painless".

How long does the procedure take?

It usually takes 1–2 sessions depending on the complexity of the case.

Do I need a crown after RCT?

Yes, in most cases a crown is recommended after root canal to protect the treated tooth.

What is the recovery time?

Most patients recover within a few days with minimal discomfort.
